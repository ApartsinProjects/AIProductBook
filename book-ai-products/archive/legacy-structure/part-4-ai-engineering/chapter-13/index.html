Chapter 13

Knowledge Retrieval and Advanced RAG

Building Reliable Knowledge Systems

Objective: Build reliable knowledge systems with advanced RAG techniques.

Chapter Overview

This chapter dives deep into knowledge retrieval systems. You will learn the vector database landscape, chunking and embedding strategies, hybrid search with reranking, advanced RAG patterns like Graph RAG and self-correcting RAG, and when to go beyond RAG to fine-tuning. These techniques are essential for any AI product that needs to access and reason over external knowledge.

The quality of your RAG system directly impacts the quality of your AI product outputs. Even the most capable model cannot produce good answers if it does not have access to the right information. This chapter provides the systematic approaches needed to build reliable knowledge retrieval systems.

Learning Objectives

  • Choose the right vector database for your use case
  • Implement effective chunking and embedding strategies
  • Build hybrid search with BM25 and dense retrieval
  • Apply advanced RAG patterns (Graph, self-correcting, RAFT)
  • Decide between RAG, fine-tuning, or both

Prerequisites

Readers should have completed Chapter 12 on orchestration. Understanding of basic ML concepts and experience with Python is expected. Familiarity with databases is helpful. If you are new to RAG, start with Section 13.1 on vector databases.
