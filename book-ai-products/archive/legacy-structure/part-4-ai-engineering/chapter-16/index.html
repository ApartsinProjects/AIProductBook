Scaling, Reliability, and Observability

Production-Hardening AI Systems

Objective: Production harden your AI systems for scale and reliability.

Chapter Overview

This chapter covers production-hardening AI systems at scale. You will learn about latency, throughput, and multi-tenancy considerations, guardrails and fallbacks for safe operation, observability tools like LangSmith and Langfuse, and security and compliance requirements including SOC2, HIPAA, and GDPR. These skills are essential for running AI products in production with confidence.

The transition from prototype to production is where many AI products fail. The techniques in this chapter address the challenges that emerge only at scale: handling high traffic, maintaining quality under load, and operating reliably over time.

Learning Objectives

  • Design for latency, throughput, and multi-tenancy at scale
  • Implement input and output guardrails
  • Build observability with tracing, metrics, and logs
  • Apply security best practices and compliance frameworks
  • Design human-in-the-loop for high-stakes decisions

Prerequisites

Readers should have completed Chapters 11-15 on AI engineering. Experience with production systems and basic security concepts is expected. If you are new to production hardening, Section 16.1 on latency and throughput provides the foundation.
