Chapter 2

The Synergy Triangle

Vibe Coding x AI PM x AI Engineering

Your team has three brilliant people working on an AI product, yet the project keeps stalling. The engineer builds features the PM never asked for. The PM specs solutions the developer cannot implement. The vibe coder ships something that no one asked for. Everyone is frustrated, and nothing ships.

This is not a talent problem. It is a coordination problem. The fix is simple: a shared framework for how the three pillars of AI product development connect and enable each other.

"The whole is greater than the sum of its parts."

Aristotle

Objective: Introduce the book's core framework and show the interdependence between the three pillars.

Chapter Overview

This chapter introduces the Synergy Triangle, the core conceptual framework that underpins the entire book. Rather than treating AI product development as a single skill, we break it into three interdependent pillars: Vibe Coding, AI Product Management, and AI Engineering. Understanding how these three pillars enable each other is essential for building products that actually ship.

The chapter covers the visual model of the triangle with its feedback loops, how each pillar enables the others, common anti-patterns, and a practical example walking through the triangle in action. By the end, you will understand how to balance investment across the three pillars and diagnose project failures using the framework.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the three pillars of the Synergy Triangle and their roles
  • Map the feedback loops between pillars
  • Apply the triangle framework to diagnose project failures
  • Balance investment across the three pillars

Prerequisites

This chapter assumes familiarity with basic software development concepts. No prior AI or ML experience is required. If you are new to AI products, we recommend reading Chapter 1 first.
