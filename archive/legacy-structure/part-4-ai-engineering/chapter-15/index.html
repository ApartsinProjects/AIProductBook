Chapter 15

Inference Serving and LLMOps

Fast and Cost-Effective AI Systems

Objective: Make systems fast and cost-effective with proper serving and LLMOps.

Chapter Overview

This chapter covers inference serving and operationalizing AI systems. You will learn serving options like vLLM, Triton, and cloud services, CI/CD practices for prompts and models, cost optimization strategies that can save 60-70%, and hybrid cloud/edge architectures. These skills are essential for running AI systems at scale profitably.

The economics of AI products often depend more on inference cost than model cost. Optimized serving can reduce your costs by half while improving latency. This chapter provides the practical knowledge needed to achieve those optimizations.

Learning Objectives

  • Choose the right serving infrastructure for your needs
  • Implement CI/CD for prompts and models
  • Apply cost optimization strategies (routing, caching, distillation)
  • Design hybrid cloud/edge architectures
  • Understand the rise of local-first and edge AI

Prerequisites

Readers should have completed Chapters 11-14 on AI engineering fundamentals. Experience with cloud infrastructure and basic DevOps concepts is expected. If you are new to serving, Section 15.1 provides an overview of options.
