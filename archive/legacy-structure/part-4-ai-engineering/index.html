AI Engineering

From Prototype to Production

Part Overview

Part IV covers the engineering depth needed to ship reliable AI products. You will build production-ready AI systems with modern orchestration, RAG, agents, and LLMOps. This part is essential for engineers who need to take vibe-coded prototypes and make them production-ready.

"Every production incident started as a vibe-coded prototype that someone forgot to harden."
