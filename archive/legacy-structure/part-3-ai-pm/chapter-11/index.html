Go-to-Market and Success Metrics

Pricing, Adoption, and Measuring AI Value

Objective: Drive real adoption and business value with the right pricing and metrics.

Chapter Overview

This chapter covers the business aspects of AI product management. You will learn six pricing models for AI products, AI-specific onboarding strategies, a metrics hierarchy beyond accuracy, and various adoption strategies for different market segments. These skills are essential for turning technical success into business success.

AI products have unique business dynamics that traditional SaaS metrics do not fully capture. High marginal costs, usage-based pricing complexity, and the challenge of communicating value for probabilistic outputs all require specialized approaches. This chapter provides frameworks for making these business decisions confidently.

Learning Objectives

  • Choose the right pricing model for your AI product
  • Design effective AI-specific onboarding experiences
  • Implement a metrics hierarchy from technical to business outcomes
  • Apply adoption strategies for developer, enterprise, and consumer markets
  • Understand unit economics in AI-first SaaS

Prerequisites

Readers should have completed Chapters 7-9 on AI PM fundamentals and ethics. Basic knowledge of SaaS pricing models and growth metrics is helpful. If you are new to GTM strategy, Section 10.1 on pricing models provides a good starting point.
