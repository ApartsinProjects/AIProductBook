Part Overview

Part III equips product managers with AI-specific tools and frameworks. You will learn eval-first strategies, cost-aware roadmapping, and responsible AI practices. Understanding how to evaluate AI products, make build/buy/bake decisions, and design for ethics will make you an invaluable member of any AI product team.
