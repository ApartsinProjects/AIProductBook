Chapter 10

Ethics, Trust, and Governance

Building Responsible AI Products

Objective: Build trustworthy products with proper governance frameworks.

Chapter Overview

This chapter addresses the critical aspects of AI ethics and governance. You will learn the NIST AI RMF and ISO 42001 frameworks, core ethical principles including bias detection and privacy, strategies for building user trust, and specific governance requirements for agentic AI systems. These topics are not optional considerations but fundamental requirements for any AI product that will be used by real people.

The governance landscape for AI products has matured significantly. Regulatory frameworks like the EU AI Act have established clear requirements, and enterprise customers increasingly demand proof of responsible AI practices before purchasing. Understanding these frameworks is not just about compliance; it is about building products that users can trust and that will stand the test of time.

Learning Objectives

  • Apply NIST AI RMF and ISO 42001 governance frameworks
  • Detect and mitigate bias in AI products
  • Implement transparency and privacy protections
  • Build user trust through honest AI behavior
  • Design governance for agentic AI systems

Prerequisites

Readers should have completed Chapters 7-8 on AI PM fundamentals. Understanding of basic AI/ML concepts and familiarity with product development processes is assumed. If you are new to governance, focus on Section 9.1 which covers the foundational frameworks.
