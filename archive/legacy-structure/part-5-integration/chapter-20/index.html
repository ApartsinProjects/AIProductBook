Future-Proofing

Trends for 2027 and Beyond

Objective: Prepare for what's next with emerging trends and personal roadmaps.

Chapter Overview

This chapter looks forward to the future of AI products. You will learn about emerging trends for 2027 including multimodal agents and robotics integration, the shift from "apps" to autonomous agents, EU AI Act compliance requirements, and how to create a personal 30/60/90-day roadmap for AI product skills. This chapter synthesizes all the learning from the book and provides a roadmap for continued growth.

The AI landscape evolves rapidly, and the frameworks in this book will need to adapt. This chapter helps you understand what is coming so you can prepare and continue to build great AI products regardless of how the technology changes.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand emerging AI trends for 2027 and beyond
  • Recognize the shift from apps to autonomous agents
  • Prepare for EU AI Act compliance
  • Create a personal AI product development roadmap
  • Apply the framework to evolving AI landscape

Prerequisites

Readers should have completed the entire book through Chapter 19. This chapter synthesizes all previous learning and looks forward. It is the culmination of your journey through AI product development.
