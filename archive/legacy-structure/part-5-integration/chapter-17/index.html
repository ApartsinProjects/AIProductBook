Integrated Vibe-to-Production Playbook

Tying It All Together

You have completed every chapter in this book. You understand AI capabilities, you can vibe code a prototype, you know how to run evals, and you can build agentic systems. Now the question is: how do you bring all of this together into a repeatable process that actually ships?

The answer is this playbook. It integrates every skill from the previous chapters into one cohesive workflow that you can apply to any AI product from concept to launch.

Objective: Tie everything together in a repeatable end-to-end process.

Chapter Overview

This chapter integrates all the previous learning into a cohesive playbook. You will learn the complete vibe-to-production workflow across four phases, practical templates and checklists for eval creation and production readiness, and cross-role collaboration patterns for PMs, AI Engineers, and Vibe Developers. This is where everything comes together.

The key insight from earlier chapters is that successful AI products require all three pillars working in concert: vibe coding for rapid prototyping, AI PM for strategy and evaluation, and AI engineering for production systems. This chapter shows you how to orchestrate all three effectively.

Learning Objectives

  • Execute the complete vibe-to-production workflow
  • Create eval checklists for your AI products
  • Apply production readiness checklists
  • Enable effective cross-role collaboration
  • Define "trust architecture" for AI products

Prerequisites

Readers should have completed Parts I-IV (Foundations, Vibe Coding, AI PM, AI Engineering) or have equivalent knowledge across all areas of AI product development. This chapter synthesizes knowledge from all previous parts.
