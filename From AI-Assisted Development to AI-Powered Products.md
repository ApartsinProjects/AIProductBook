## Book Description

This book bridges the gap between rapid **vibe coding** (intent-driven AI-assisted development) and production-grade **AI-powered products**. It equips readers with a synergistic framework combining:

- **Vibe Coding**: High-velocity prototyping with tools like Cursor, Claude Code, and emerging agentic IDEs.
- **AI Product Management**: Eval-first strategies, cost-aware roadmapping, and responsible AI practices tailored for probabilistic systems.
- **AI Engineering**: Modern 2026 tech stack for orchestration, RAG, agents, LLMOps, and reliable deployment.

The result? A complete, no-hype path from idea validation to live, observable, and monetizable AI products. Every section emphasizes **synergy**: how vibe coding accelerates PM experimentation, how PM-defined evals guide engineering choices, and how the engineering stack turns prototypes into trustworthy systems.

**Key Features:**

- Hands-on examples, decision frameworks, checklists, and templates.
- 2026-updated coverage of agentic workflows, multi-agent orchestration (MCP, A2A protocols), inference economics, multimodal capabilities, and hybrid architectures.
- Real-world case studies from vertical agents, copilots, and autonomous systems.
- Three reading tracks: Developer, PM, and Founder.
